    TransactionKind

    Possible kind of transaction.

    Attributes:

    • ARTIFACT –

      Artifact items from domains.

    • CRYSTAL –

      Genesis crystal currency.

    • PRIMOGEM –

      Primogem currency.

    • RESIN –

      Resin currency.

    • WEAPON –

      Weapon items from domains and wishes.
